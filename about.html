KevSchool.org is an upcoming nonprofit whose goal is to create and maintain free educational resources on the Internet.

The KevSchool List of Educational Web Searches is the first project by KevSchool.org.
It consists of a list of educational topics that can be searched for on the Internet.
This is a very simple app, but can be very educational.
Over time, many more topics and categories will be added.

KevSchool.org was created by Kevin Verre.

To suggest topics to be added to the list, please contact Kevin.
